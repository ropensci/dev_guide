2018-06-06

Preface

This book has the ambition to become a guide for maintainers of rOpenSci packages, in particular volunteers who submit a package to onboarding.

The first section of the book presents our guidelines for building and testing your package.

The second section is dedicated to onboarding: what it is, our policies, and specific guides for authors, editors and reviewers.

The third and last section features our best practice for nurturing your package: how to collaborate with other developers, how to document releases, how to promote your package and how to leverage GitHub as a development platform.

We hope that you’ll find the guide useful and clear, and welcome your suggestions in the issue tracker of the book. Happy R packaging!

The rOpenSci editorial team.
